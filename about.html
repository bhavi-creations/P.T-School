About Our Founder

God has blessed this PTS and many aspects have been added to the school.

In 1976 we started with the Telugu medium. In addition to our major objective of Bible training, the Carl Technical School with Typing Practice, computer operation, TV and radio repairs, tailoring, and others. Courses were started for the benefit of students to learn “how to live and how to make living” while they started self-supporting congregations. The PTS provides living assistance to preachers for only 5 years and then they need to be self-supporting.

In 1991 we started B.B.S (B.D) 3 years English medium Bible training. This allows us to accept and teach people from all the states of India. Along with this we started night school for local people.

In 1995 the school began offering a 30 lesson of a Bible correspondence course in both English and Telugu. This can help anyone in the population.

In 2000 we were able to establish a full time Bible Training for women. This is known as the Calvin Women Bible Training. This course is designed for one year and two years. Preachers need to have their wife trained to help her husband interact with the Indian culture between men and women.

In 2002 we were able to start Monthly 5-day school for all who are working full time in preaching including preachers who graduated from the PTS and Pastors in the denominations. This is a wonderful way to reach the denominational churches and we were blessed by this work to change the whole congregation.

In 2022 August we were able to start a Distance Education for people who are not able to attend school on a regular basis. This course is designed for 4 semesters. On the end of the each semester they will write exams in the school premises and after 4 semesters work they will B.Th; degree.

Dr.K. G. Kumar

The Founder President and Director of the Schools is Kedarisetti Satyanarayana known as Kedarisetti Gershon Kumar aged 76 years. He is also the founder of the schools. In 1976 he graduated from Freed-Hardeman University with an AA and BS degree. Upon graduation Kumar came back to India his native country on July 14th. The Humboldt Church of Christ in Tennessee was the sponsoring congregationuntil June 2000. From July 2000 Northeast at Kingsport, TN USA has taken over the whole work along with other supporters. This Preachers Training School (PTS) was established and started on August 1976 with Telugu Medium (language), the state language of Andhra Pradesh.

Kumar was born Hindu in a Vyssya family. He accepted Jesus Christ as hispersonal Saviour on 1st January 1970 and was baptized by Dr. J.C. Bailey from Canada. Then Freed-Hardeman College offered him a full scholarship to study Bible by its president Dr. E.Claude Gardner. He was admitted in August 1972 for 4 years of Bible study and degree. After several decades of work at the PTS, Freed Hardeman awarded Kumar an honorary Doctor’s degree May 10, 2003 for his life’s work.

Dr. Kumar is the author of following books in Telugu:

  • Critical Introduction to New and Old Testaments (2001)
  • Marriage and Family(2002)
  • The Church History(2004)
  • Preacher’s Handbook(2005)
  • Commentary on Epistle Jude(2010)
  • Verse by verse commentary on Romans(2020)
